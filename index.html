Allergy & Nutritional Info

Food Allergy Warning:

Our cookies are made onsite and may come into contact with different allergens during production. Please be advised that any of our products may contain allergens including peanuts, tree nuts, milk, eggs, wheat, soy, and sesame. For additional nutritional information, please visit crumblcookies.com/nutrition.
